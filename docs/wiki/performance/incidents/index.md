## Incident Severity Levels

| Level | Definition | Response Time |
|-------|------------|---------------|
| **Critical** | System-wide impact, users unable to work | Immediate |
| **High** | Significant performance degradation | < 1 hour |
| **Medium** | Noticeable impact, workarounds available | < 4 hours |
| **Low** | Minor impact, isolated issue | Next business day |

---

## Post-Incident Review Process

### For All Incidents
1. **Document timeline** - What happened and when
2. **Identify root cause** - Why it happened
3. **Assess impact** - Who was affected, for how long
4. **Define prevention** - How to prevent recurrence
5. **Track follow-ups** - Ensure actions are completed
